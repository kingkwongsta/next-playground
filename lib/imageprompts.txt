Cocktail

In the center of the bar, illuminated by a spotlight emanating from the ceiling, sits a masterpiece of cocktail. A crystal coupe glass cradles a ruby-red concoction. Fresh strawberries glisten atop the frothy surface, crowned with a sprig of mint. Dew clings to the rim, hinting at the potent depths within. Citrus twists spiral elegantly within the shimmering liquid, promising a tantalizing dance of sweet and smoky flavors.
Utilizing photorealistic and hyper-detailed style to capture the rich textures and vibrant colors of the scene. Additionally emphasize the interplay of light and shadow, creating a sense of drama and intrigue.
